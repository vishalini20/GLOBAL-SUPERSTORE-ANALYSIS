# 🌍 Global Superstore Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Global Superstore Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Tableau project designed to analyze sales, profit, product performance, regional trends, and customer purchasing patterns across multiple global markets. The dashboard transforms raw retail data into actionable business insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, and interactive filters.

## 🎯 Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to monitor business performance, identify profitable regions and product categories, analyze sales trends, and support data-driven decision-making through interactive reporting.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Region Wise Sales
• Displays total sales generated across global regions.  
• Compares sales performance among regions.  
• Identifies top-performing and low-performing markets.

### Sub-Category Wise Sales
• Analyzes sales contribution by product sub-category.  
• Highlights best-selling and least-performing products.  
• Supports inventory and product strategy decisions.

### Month Wise Quantity Sold
• Tracks quantity sold across different months.  
• Identifies seasonal demand patterns.  
• Helps forecast future inventory requirements.

### Monthly Sales Analysis
• Displays monthly sales performance trends.  
• Identifies peak and low sales periods.  
• Supports strategic sales planning.

### Region Wise Profit & Sales
• Visualizes the relationship between profit and sales across regions.  
• Highlights regions generating maximum profitability.  
• Enables comparative regional analysis.

### Category Wise Sales
• Shows percentage contribution of product categories to overall sales.  
• Helps identify high-performing categories.  
• Supports category management decisions.

### Category Wise Profit
• Compares profit generated by different categories.  
• Highlights profitable and underperforming categories.  
• Supports profitability optimization strategies.

## 🎛️ Interactive Filters

## 🔍 Key Insights

• Central region generates the highest sales revenue.  
• December records the highest monthly sales performance.  
• Sales volume increases significantly during the final quarter of the year.  
• Technology-related sub-categories contribute strongly to overall sales.  
• Profitability varies across regions and product categories.  
• Certain categories generate high sales but comparatively lower profit margins.  
• Regional performance analysis helps identify expansion opportunities.
